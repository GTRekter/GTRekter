## Hello World

Ever since I was a little kid, technology and videogames have been a fundamental part of my life. I still remember sitting in front of an Amiga playing Cave Story. It was during middle school that I grew hungry to learn more. Even now that I'm working as a Senior Software Developer, I can't stop taking professional courses on topics from Artificial Intelligence to CyberSecurity. IT is a never-ending update; there is always something new to learn, like learning to set up a Raspberry Pi after seeing Mr. Robot and hacking my wife's computer. 😁 (Don't worry, I bought her a subscription to Norton after that.)

I have extensive experience in web application development and software development life cycles. Using this experience, along with my knowledge of user experience design, I create solutions that not only work well but work right, by writing code that respects the SOLID principles.

During my free time, I enjoy sharing my knowledge and experiences with the development community by writing articles on LinkedIn and Medium as well as helping other developers by replying to their questions on StackOverflow.

While I love coding in general, I am a Microsoft stack man, and I want to stay loyal to my tribe.
